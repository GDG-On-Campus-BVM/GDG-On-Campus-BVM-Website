Events Catalogue

A gist of Events at our Google Developer Student Club Chapter - BVM.

April 15, 2023 Offline

Animals and animal welfare


Dr. Bhavesh Solanki addressed that Animal welfare refers to the ethical and humane treatment of animals, with the goal of ensuring their well-being and minimizing any suffering they may experience.

March 18, 2023 Offline

Premiere Pro Unleashed - Master Premiere Pro Like A Pro


On 18th March' 23 , GDSC BVM had organized an informational session on video editing in Adobe Premiere Pro, the session was conducted by Er. Vatsal Joshi.

March 18, 2023 Industry Visit

An Industry visit Report at INDO GERMAN TOOL ROOM & PRSSUREJET SYSTEM Pvt. Ltd.


An Industry visit at INDO GERMAN TOOL ROOM & PRSSUREJET SYSTEM Pvt. Ltd on 18th of march of 2023.

March 12, 2023 Virtual

Flutter Forward Extended


The speaker, Ayush Gaur(Flutter Lead, GDSC BITD) started the session with a basic introduction of Flutter, how it works and how is it useful. He also explained some of the main features of Flutter and Dart and how to make interesting and eye-catching interfaces.

March 11, 2023 Workshop

Excel Skills for Numerical Methods


The sessions were conducted on 11th March, 2023. 35 participants had attended the workshop which includes students of 2nd year Civil, Mechanical and Computer Engineering branch. Some faculties also attended the workshop. The workshop was coordinated by Dr. Dipti R. Patel from Mathematics department.

March 4, 2023 Virtual

Hues of Code - A coding competition


On 4th March, 2023, Google Developer Student Clubs - BVM and CodeChef BVM Chapter organized an online coding competition “Hues of Code”. Coding enthusiastic participants were given 6 problem statements to solve in one hour and thirty minutes.

March 4, 2023 Technical Visit

Technical visit to L&T-CSTI and Science Carnival 2023, Ahmedabad


The study of structural and functional components of a building is of vital importance for the students, and an overview of construction practices and techniques with some industrial exposure is a mandate for being technically sound.

March 4, 2023 Offline

Applications of Linear Algebra & Fourier series in Engineering


IQAC BVM & Birla Vishvakarma Mahavidyalaya organized an Expert talk on “Applications of Linear Algebra & Fourier series in Engineering” on 25rd February 2023 with the collaboration of GDSC from AICTE SPICES Scheme and BVM Alumni Association

February 4 and 11, 2023 Workshop

WebLinguistics-(React JS Workshop)


On 4th and 11th of February, 2023, Google Developer Student Clubs BVM and GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter Club BVM - BVM organized a hands on Workshop on React JS in which all the participants were introduced to the React JS framework and how to implement it in your Project.

January 28, 2023 GCSC Visit

A GALACTIC ODYSSEY TO GUJARAT COUNCIL OF SCIENCE CITY (GCSC)


The tour not only added a lot to the participants’ knowledge of space and science but also gave them a new perspective on things they were already familiar with. All the while, they had fun and made lasting memories!

January 27, 2023 Industry Visit

An Industry visit at Einfochips


An Industry visit at Einfochips on 27th of January of 2023. After visiting the eInfochips-An Arrow Company at ahmedabad, students had come across with the newly introduced technology and innovations in the core sector of the electronics and communication engineering.

December 9 and 12, 2022 Workshop

Hands On IoT


On 9th and 10th of December 2022, GDSC BVM had organized a Hands-On Session on IoT(Internet Of Things), the session was conducted by Er. Keyur Chauhan .IoT being one of the most advancing technologies of today's era, the two-days workshop was organized to help students know more about it and perform some experiments with the same.

November 5, 2022 Virtual

INTRO TO CLOUD AND GCCP PROGRAM


On 5th November, 2022, Google Developer Student Clubs - BVM organized an online Info Session “INTRO TO CLOUD AND GCCP PROGRAM in which Our Core Member Give Information about Google Cloud Career Practitioner” (GCCP). and Related Exams Sessions.

October 8, 2022 Offline

Hands-on Python


On 8th October 2022, GDSC BVM in collaboration with Women development cell BVM had organized a Hands-on session on Python for female candidates only. The session started with a welcome/introductory note by Dr. Zankhana Shah, Women Development Cell officer and Dr. Trupti Desai in the presence of Dr. Darshak G.Thakore, Dr.Vatsal Shah and Prof. Bijal Dalwadi. Then the session was handed over to the speakers.

September 17, 2022 Industry Visit

An Industry visit at JYOTI CNC AUTOMATION Pvt. Ltd.


Outcomes : We saw flexible manufacturing system which is helpful for our course, We also saw foundry shop which we had studied during our previous years, We also saw different types of CNC machines and their codes which we studied during our CAM subject

Edging Towards a Connected Future


Its goal was to raise awareness of various trending technologies which will connect us to a sustainable future, while learning more about them from speakers from illustrious organisations like ISRO, IPR, Forensic Cybertech etc.

Kickstart Session on AWS


On 2nd September 2022, GDSC-BVM organized a Kickstart Session on AWS. It was an introductory session for AWS and the certifications provided by AWS.
