Welcome

What is GDSC ?

Helping students bridge the gap between Theory and Practice.

Google Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

Events

Events Catalogue

A gist of Events at our Google Developer Student Club Chapter - BVM.

July 31, 2024   Offline

GDSC Induction


The induction program introduced freshers to the activities, values, and opportunities of the Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) at BVM. It aimed to foster community, encourage participation, and provide an engaging introduction to the club's initiatives through interactive sessions, games, and direct communication with the GDSC lead.

July 27, 2024   Offline

Expert Talk on DevOPS Fundamentals


The Expert Session on "DevOPS Fundamentals" aimed to provide final-year students with a clear understanding of DevOps principles, practices, and tools. It focused on equipping students with practical DevOps skills essential for modern software development and deployment, bridging the gap between academic learning and industry practices.

May 1, 2024   Online

Generative AI Study Jam


GDSC BVM presents the 30-Days Gen AI Study Jam, an exciting opportunity to dive into the world of Generative AI! This event offers hands-on workshops, facilitator-led sessions, and a chance to earn Google certifications. Enhance your AI skills, engage with a passionate community, and take your knowledge to the next level—all for free!

February 10, 2024

Solution Fest 2.0


It is an intra-college Hackathon, a stepping stone for the ultimate Google Solution Challenge. Together, let's code, create, and change the world, one solution at a time! See you there, Future Innovators!🌟💻

January 11, 2024  Offline

Solution Eve


Solution Eve is your ticket to the galaxy of Google hackathon as in this event we are going to launch GDSC BVM's flagship hackathon Solution Fest 2.0- Where Every Challenge has a Solution.
