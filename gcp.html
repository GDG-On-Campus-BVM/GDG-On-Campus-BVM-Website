About 30 Days of Google Cloud!

30 Days of Google Cloud program provides an opportunity to kickstart your career in cloud and get hands-on practice on Google Cloud - the tool that powers apps like Google Search, Gmail and YouTube.

Along the way, you will learn & practice concepts like computing, application development, big data & machine learning using cloud.
